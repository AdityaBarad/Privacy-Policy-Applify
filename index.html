# Privacy Policy for Applify **Last Updated: April 13, 2025** ## Introduction Thank you for choosing Applify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our browser extension. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through the Applify extension. ## Information We Collect Applify collects the following types of information to provide and improve our job application automation service: ### Personal Information - **Job Application Data**: Information you provide for job applications, such as: - Name - Email address - Phone number - Resume information - Work experience - Education details - Skills - **Search Preferences**: Job search criteria including keywords, location preferences, experience level, and salary expectations ### Non-Personal Information - **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the extension, including: - Number of applications submitted - Job sites visited - Features used within the extension - Error reports - **Performance Data**: Technical information that helps us maintain and improve the extension ## How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect for the following purposes: 1. **Provide Core Functionality**: To automate job applications across supported platforms (LinkedIn, Indeed, Unstop, Internshala, and Naukri) 2. **Store Your Preferences**: To remember your search criteria and application preferences 3. **Track Application Progress**: To maintain records of your job applications 4. **Improve Our Services**: To understand usage patterns and enhance the extension's functionality 5. **Technical Support**: To diagnose problems and provide assistance ## Data Storage and Security - **Local Storage**: Most of your data is stored locally on your device using the browser's storage API - **Security Measures**: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data - **Data Retention**: We only retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services ## Third-Party Access Applify does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We do not share your personal data with any third-party job sites beyond what is necessary to complete job applications on your behalf. The only data transmitted to job sites are the details you've provided for completing job applications, and this is done solely for the purpose of submitting applications on your behalf. ## Your Choices and Rights You have several rights regarding your personal information: 1. **Access**: You can access your stored information through the extension settings 2. **Correction**: You can update your information at any time 3. **Deletion**: You can clear all stored data within the extension settings 4. **Opt-Out**: You can uninstall the extension at any time to stop all data collection ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. ## Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: Email: support@applify-extension.com ## Consent By installing and using the Applify extension, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
